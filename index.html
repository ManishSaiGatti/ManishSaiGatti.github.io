About Assassin's Creed Origins

Setting

Assassin's Creed Origins is set in ancient Egypt during the Ptolemaic period. Players will explore a vast open world that includes cities, deserts, and the Nile River.

Main Character(s)

The main character in Assassin's Creed Origins is Bayek, a Medjay warrior who seeks revenge against those who harmed his family. Players will also encounter other important characters throughout the game.

Gameplay

Assassin's Creed Origins features a blend of stealth, combat, and exploration. Players can engage in intense combat using a variety of weapons and abilities. The game also introduces a new leveling system and RPG elements, allowing players to customize their character's skills and equipment.
